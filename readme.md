# Awesome Technical Accounting [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of high-quality resources for technical accounting and financial reporting under US GAAP and IFRS.

Technical accounting is the practice of researching, interpreting, and applying accounting standards to complex transactions and preparing compliant financial statements. This list collects authoritative standards, the major firms' freely available interpretive guidance, and the best independent newsletters, blogs, and learning resources in the space.

## Standards & Authoritative Sources

- [FASB Accounting Standards Codification](https://asc.fasb.org/) - The single source of authoritative US GAAP, searchable online (free Basic View).
- [Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB)](https://www.fasb.org/) - Standard setter for US GAAP; publishes Accounting Standards Updates, exposure drafts, and project updates.
- [IFRS Foundation (IASB)](https://www.ifrs.org/) - Issuer of IFRS Accounting Standards used in 140+ jurisdictions, with a free register of issued standards.
- [SEC EDGAR Full-Text Search](https://www.sec.gov/edgar/search/) - Full-text search across public company filings; the primary corpus for disclosure research and benchmarking.
- [SEC Financial Reporting Manual](https://www.sec.gov/about/divisions-offices/division-corporation-finance/financial-reporting-manual) - Division of Corporation Finance staff guidance on SEC financial statement requirements.
- [SEC Staff Accounting Bulletins](https://www.sec.gov/rules-regulations/staff-guidance/staff-accounting-bulletins) - SEC staff interpretations on accounting and disclosure (e.g., materiality and revenue).
- [AICPA & CIMA](https://www.aicpa-cima.com/) - Audit and accounting guides, Technical Questions and Answers, and the Center for Plain English Accounting.


## Firm Reference Libraries

Free hubs where the major firms publish their interpretive accounting guidance.

- [BDO Accounting Standards & Reporting Matters](https://www.bdo.com/accounting-and-sec-matters) - Blueprints, bulletins, and newsletters on US GAAP and SEC developments.
- [Deloitte Accounting Research Tool (DART)](https://dart.deloitte.com/) - Deloitte's Roadmap series and manuals, much of it free with registration.
- [Deloitte IAS Plus](https://www.iasplus.com/) - Free global IFRS and US GAAP news, standards summaries, and resources.
- [EY AccountingLink](https://www.ey.com/en_us/technical/accountinglink) - EY's Financial Reporting Developments publications, Technical Line guidance, and quarterly briefs.
- [Grant Thornton Insights](https://www.grantthornton.com/insights) - Accounting and reporting bulletins, New Developments Summaries, and quarterly updates.
- [KPMG Financial Reporting View](https://kpmg.com/us/en/frv.html) - KPMG's hub of handbooks, hot topics, and CPE-eligible insights on US GAAP, IFRS, and SEC reporting.
- [PwC Viewpoint](https://viewpoint.pwc.com/) - PwC's accounting and reporting guides, In briefs, and In depths (free registration).
- [RSM Financial Reporting Resource Center](https://rsmus.com/insights/financial-reporting.html) - Bi-weekly Financial Reporting Insights and topic-organized accounting guidance.
